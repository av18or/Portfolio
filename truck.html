1998 Toyota Tacoma project

  • Vehicle: 1998 Toyota Tacoma SR5 4x4 manual.

  • Engine: 5VZ-FE 3.4L V6.

  • Front bumper: Chassis Unlimited "octane winch bumper".

  • Lighting: Amber bumper fog lights - generic.

  • Skid plates: Skid Row Offroad.

  • Suspension-Front: Bilstein 5100 shocks with OME coils.

  • Suspension-Rear: Bilstein 5100's with All-Pro Offroad Expedition leaf springs.

  • Wheels: Mickey Thompson Classics.

  • Tires: Mickey Thompson Baja ATZ P3 31x10.5 r15.

  • Grille: 1st gen TRD style with raptor lights.

  • Bed rack: CBI Offroad Fab overland bed bars.

  • Rooftop tent: CVT Mt. Shasta.

Maintenance log

I keep a fairly meticulous list of all maintenance items for any vehicle I own, and this Tacoma is no exception. I chose this vehicle mainly because it had spent it's entire life in a desert climate.
This being important after researching the history of certain Tacoma's and frame rust issues.
After spending about a year searching, I found this truck on Craigslist. I verified it's history using a CarFax report, combined with information from the owner, before making the decision to purchase. I knew full well the truck would need work, but it would have been helpful to have some kind of resource to know what items tend to need replacing on a Tacoma of this age.
